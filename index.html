About Us

The Highland Springs Village Golfing Community is made up of three separate player clubs. The Men's Club and Women's Club are clubs comprised of players of all skill levels, with the requirement being that members be a resident of Highland Springs Village.

The Travelling Golf Club, which is for both men and women aged 55 and above, whether they be residents and non-residents, has monthly golf tournaments at different locations each month.
